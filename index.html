About IFW

IFW is more than just a funding program — it's a movement built to uplift individuals, families, and communities through strategic financial empowerment.

Our mission is simple but powerful: to identify promising individuals and provide them with real opportunities to move forward — starting with a minimum financial grant of $10,000. But we don’t stop there. We offer mentorship, education, and ongoing support to ensure every dollar becomes a stepping stone to success.
